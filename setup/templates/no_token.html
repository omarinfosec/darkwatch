Where the token lives

On the VM:

sudo grep ^SETUP_AUTH_TOKEN= /var/lib/darkwebapp/env

Why a token at all?

This UI can write VPN private keys and Telegram credentials to disk and restart services. The token plus your network gating (SSH tunnel, your own VPN, or a reverse proxy with auth) keeps it from being open to the internet.
